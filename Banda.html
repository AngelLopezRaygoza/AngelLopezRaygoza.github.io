What is Banda?

In this Genre of Regional Mexican Music, the intruments that are typically used, are Clarinets, Tubas, Trumpets, Charchetas(Alto Horns), ect. As well as Tamboras(Two Headed Drums) and Tarolas(Snare Drums).
