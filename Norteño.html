Norteño

What is Norteño?

In this Genre of Regional Mexican Music, the intruments that are typically used, are La Bateria(Drum Set), Electric Bass, Bajo Quinto/or Sexto(Five/or Six Stringed Bass), and the Accordian. With the Saxophone sometines thrown in cause why not.

Who/What Are Some Artists/Bands?

Some artists/bands that are in this Genre of music are, Los Tucanes de Tijuana, Grupo Codiciado, Los Cadetes de Linares, Los Invasores de Nuevo León, Grupo Intocable.
