Mariachi

What is Mariachi?

In this Genre of Regional Mexican Music, the intruments that are typically used, are Classical Guitars, Violins, Trumpets, Vihuelas, and Guitarrónes.

Who/What Are Some Artists/Bands?

Some artists/bands that are in this Genre of music are, Vicente Fernández, Aldo Trujillo, Juan Gabriel, and José Alfredo Jiménez
