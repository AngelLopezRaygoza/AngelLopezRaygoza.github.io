Corridos Tumbados

What are Corridos Tumbados?

Corridos Tumbados is pretty much the same as Campirano, as they both use the same instruments. The 12-String Guitar(Lead), 6-String Guitar(Harmony), and the Tuba (Bass). But in Corridos Tumbados the Electric Bass and Tololoche(Double Bass), are commonly interchanged with the Tuba. What really sets them apart though is that they both have their unique styles, which are easier to show then explain.

Who/What Are Some Artists/Bands?

Some artists/bands that are in this Genre of music are, Herencia de Patrones, Los del Puerto, Aldo Trujillo, Junior H, and Fuerza Regida.
