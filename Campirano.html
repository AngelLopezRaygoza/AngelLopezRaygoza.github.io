What is Campirano?

In this Genre of Regional Mexican Music, the intruments that are typically used, are the 12-String Guitar(Lead), 6-String Guitar(Harmony), and the Tuba(Bass). The Tuba Sometimes being interchanged with the Bass Guitar.
