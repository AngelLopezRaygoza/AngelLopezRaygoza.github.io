Regional Mexican Music Genres

What is Regional Mexican Music?

Regional Mexican is a Latin music radio format encompassing the musical genres from the various parts of rural Mexico and the Southwestern United States. Genres include Campirano, Corridos Tumbados, Banda, Norteño, Grupero, Mariachi and others.
